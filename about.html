A founders' note

Respect,
the noun.

Chris & Kelsey Olson on why a Third Wave cafe belongs in the Levee District, how the name arrived, and what cozy elegance actually means to us.

Respect.

For the bean, the farmer, the roaster, the barista, and the person on the other side of the counter.

Local.

Built in East Peoria, by East Peoria locals — for the neighbors we share streets, sidewalks, and mornings with.

Coffee.

Carefully sourced specialty coffee, finely tuned by baristas who care about the last five grams.

We're Chris & Kelsey Olson — brand new business owners with a passion for Third Wave coffee and a motivation to bring it to the Levee District.

As a family, we enjoy experiencing local coffee shops wherever we are. Being East Peoria locals ourselves, we felt a void of this in our city. So, the idea arose — and here we are.

Where "Reverence" came from.

Our mindset here is to respect coffee and show off all the amazing — and sometimes exotic — qualities it can have. Looking for synonyms, we came across "Reverence." With such a strong feel to the word, we were immediately drawn to it, and the brand and concept followed.

More than
a beverage.

Coffee, locally.

We strive to create an exceptional coffee experience that transcends the ordinary — on a local scale. Through our dedication to sourcing beans from renowned roasteries, we curate a diverse and exquisite selection of specialty coffees that cater to every palate. Our experienced baristas bring those specifically curated beans to life.

Cozy elegance.

Our warm atmosphere provides a sanctuary where patrons can savor their coffee amidst the harmonious interplay of comfort and sophistication — a term we coined for our design concept: cozy elegance. We believe a cup of coffee should be more than a beverage; it should be an experience that enriches your senses and soothes your soul.

Slow down and enjoy the company you're with, the beverage of your choice, and a beautiful atmosphere.

Bring people together.

Our goal is to bring locals together — whether that's with friends, family, coworkers, or clients. We hope to help you slow down and enjoy the company you're with, your beverage of choice, and a beautiful atmosphere.

We look forward to seeing you soon.

— Chris & Kelsey
